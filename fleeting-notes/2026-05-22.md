## 05:20 UTC

**[Herdr](https://herdr.dev/)** — Love this. Terminal-native agent runtime with tmux-style persistence, mouse-native panes, and semantic agent state. Works so well in Termux.

## 18:22 UTC

Still using Herdr daily. Tried [cmux](https://github.com/x-cmux/cmux) for a bit but it doesn't persist sessions on restarts — which feels like the whole point? Herdr feels more like tmux on easy mode. Love how it just works over SSH too. Feels more like a TUI Warp.

## 07:45 UTC

**[macOS Caffeinate Command](https://james.brooks.page/blog/macos-caffeinate-command)** — Stop your laptop from sleeping with `caffeinate`, perfect for letting your agents run!

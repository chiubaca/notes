### Navigation

- Basic: `h` (left), `j` (down), `k` (up), `l` (right)
- Words: `w` (next word), `b` (previous word), `e` (end of word)
- Lines: `0` (start), `^` (first non-whitespace), `$` (end)
- Scrolling: `Ctrl+d` (down), `Ctrl+u` (up)
- File: `gg` (top), `G` (bottom)
- Go to line  `:[line-number]`

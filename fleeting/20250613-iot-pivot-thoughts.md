# Thinking about pivoting to IoT and skilling up as an electrician

2026-06-13

Day 1 of the career pivot journey. Feeling a mix of uncertainty and excitement.

- AI is moving fast — day job is mostly planning, prompting, and reviewing AI code now. Not the same fulfillment as writing software myself.
- Want to work with physical systems, not just screens.
- The electrical trade landscape is clear: ELV / Systems Integration is the only path that makes sense for a senior dev with 15 years of logic work and a toddler.
- 80% logic / 20% labor — that's the leverage.
- Need to start with the theoretical foundation: structured cabling, PoE, networking, protocols.
- The garage mock-up wall feels like the first tangible milestone.
- CIT exam is the first credential to aim for.

Key tension: am I running *away* from AI anxiety, or *toward* something I actually want? Probably both. Need to keep checking that.
